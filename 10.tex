Let $A_{n}=\left[0,\frac{1}{n}\right)$. As we know $\cap_{n=1}^{\infty}\left(0,\frac{1}{n}\right)=\emptyset$. But for $A_{n}$ greatest lower bound is 0, so $\forall n [0 \in A_{n}]$. That satisfy given properties.
